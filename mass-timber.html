Building Codes & Certifications

Mass timber construction is now recognized in all major building codes. The 2021 International Building Code (IBC) introduced three new construction types specifically for tall mass timber buildings:

Type IV-A

18 Stories

Non-combustible protection on all mass timber elements

Type IV-B

12 Stories

Limited exposed wood ceilings allowed

Type IV-C

9 Stories

All wood surfaces may be exposed
