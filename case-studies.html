Case Study #1

Vermont Mixed Hardwood Estate

A 500-acre multi-generational family forest generates new revenue streams while preserving legacy values.

Carbon Credits LiDAR Survey Estate Planning

The Challenge

The Henderson family had owned this Vermont woodlot for four generations but faced significant estate tax exposure. They wanted to generate income without compromising the forest they'd carefully stewarded for over 80 years.

Our Solution

We conducted a comprehensive LiDAR survey that revealed 23% more merchantable timber than previous estimates, including a 40-acre section of valuable black walnut. We enrolled the property in our carbon credit program while developing a selective harvest plan for the most valuable timber.

Case Study #3

Mass Timber Office Tower

A 12-story Portland office building achieves LEED Platinum using our CLT panels with full carbon tracking.

CLT Panels LEED Platinum Carbon Neutral

The Challenge

A commercial developer wanted to construct a carbon-neutral office tower using mass timber. They needed a CLT supplier who could provide both the technical specifications for a tall wood building and the carbon documentation for LEED certification.

Our Solution

We supplied 45,000 square feet of 5-ply CLT floor panels and glulam columns with CNC-machined connection points. Our engineering team collaborated with their architects on fire rating details and lateral load systems. Complete carbon accounting documented 2,400 tons of CO2 stored in the structure.
